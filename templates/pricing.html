Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your billing page. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.

What happens if I hit my loan limit?

On the Free plan, you can have up to 3 active loans. If you reach the limit, you'll need to upgrade to Basic (25 loans) or Pro (unlimited) to create more.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Start any paid plan (Basic or Pro) with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required upfront. Cancel anytime during the trial at no charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe, our secure payment processor.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription at any time from the billing page. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
